General rules for Discord

1) Be respectful
Treat all members with respect. Discriminatory, threatening, or offensive behavior is not allowed. Remember, there are people behind the screens.

2) No spam or self-promotion
Spamming messages, links, or images is prohibited. Self-promotion of your own channel, server, or social media without permission from an administrator is not allowed.

3) Hold det relevant
Sørg for, at dine beskeder er relevante for det specifikke kanal-omraade. Brug de dedikerede kanaler til deres formål.

4) Keep it relevant
Make sure your messages are relevant to the specific channel area. Use the dedicated channels for their intended purpose.

5) Language and content
Use appropriate language. Gossip, hate speech, racism, sexual harassment, or any other inappropriate content will not be tolerated.

6) No spam bots or automated tools
Do not use bots or automated tools without permission from the server administrators.

7) No trolling or bullying
Trolling, bullying, or creating drama with the intention of upsetting other members will result in a warning or ban.

8) Respect for privacy
Do not share others' personal information (doxxing). Respect others' privacy both on and off the server.

9) Follow Discord's community guidelines
Abide by Discord's official community rules and terms of service (which can be found on Discord's website).

10 Follow the management's instructions
Listen to the server's administrators and moderators. If you receive a warning or sanction, respect the decision.

 Sanctions for violations
Warning > The first violation will result in a friendly reminder.
Temporary ban > Repeated or serious violations may lead to a temporary exclusion.
Permanent ban > Serious or repeated violations will result in a permanent ban from the server.

Frequently Asked Questions:
		

 How do I share the Discord?
Join our Discordher.

How do I become Admin?
You can request an Admin from an Admin if you have a valid reason. An application will then be created, which must be approved by the Head Admins.

Can I speak in languages other than English and Danish??
It is not allowed to speak foreign languages in the community with others, as it may irritate others.
Speaking in a foreign language may result in a MUTE without warning.

Who should I contact for help?
You can contact an Admin by writing in the #call-admin text channel. If this doesn't work, you can contact an Admin via their Steam.
You can find their direct Steam by clicking on their name under the "Staff" section.
There should usually be an Admin online in most cases.
